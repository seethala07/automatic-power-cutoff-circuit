# Automatic Power Cutoff Circuit

## Overview
Designed and developed an automatic power cutoff circuit to disconnect electrical supply when unsafe operating conditions are detected.

## Objective
To improve electrical safety by preventing overload, overheating, and unnecessary power consumption.

## Features
- Automatic power disconnection
- Threshold-based protection
- Relay-controlled switching
- Improved device safety

## Components Used
- Relay
- Resistors
- Capacitors
- Power Supply
- Connecting Wires
- Electronic Components (as applicable)

## Skills Applied
- Circuit Design
- Basic Electronics
- Relay Control
- Testing & Troubleshooting

## Outcome
Successfully developed a working protection circuit capable of automatically cutting off power under predefined conditions to enhance system reliability and safety.
